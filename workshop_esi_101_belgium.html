Energy Systems Integration 101

KU Leuven, Leuven, Belgium
May 18–22, 2015

The Energy Systems Integration 101 workshop was held May 18â€“22, 2015, at KU Leuven in Belgium. This introductory course covered the concept of energy systems integration (ESI) at all scales (from residential to continental) across multiple energy (and non-energy) domains from a technical, market, and regulatory perspective. The course was designed for graduate students and practitioners working in the field to give a perspective on the state of the art in ESI.
