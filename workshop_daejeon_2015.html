ICT Enabling Thermal Energy Flexibility in Integrated Energy Systems

Korea Institute of Energy Research, Daejeon, Korea
October 21â€“22, 2015

The Information and Communications Technologies (ICT) Enabling Thermal Energy Flexibility in Integrated Energy Systems workshop was held October 21â€“22, 2015, at the Korea Institute of Energy Research (KIER) in Daejeon, Korea. The workshop included participants from Korea, Japan, Europe, and the United States and enabled the sharing of international experience on energy systems integration (ESI) with a focus on ICT-enabled heat and electricity systems. Presentations and discussions covered commercial operation, business strategy, and research.
