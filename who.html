Who We Are

The International Institute for Energy Systems Integration (iiESI) is a global institute aimed at solving energy challenges through collaboration and education on energy systems integration.

Current Members

Imperial College London
Technical University of Denmark (DTU)
The Electric Power Research Institute

Our Mission

Through knowledge capture, management, and transfer, iiESI will ensure that investments in energy systems integration are coordinated and optimized to yield the greatest value possible to the global community.

Our Vision

Our vision is a global community of scholars and practitioners from leading institutions engaged in efforts to enable highly integrated, flexible, clean, and efficient energy systems.

Near-Term Objectives

The near-term objectives of the International Institute for Energy Systems Integration are to:

  • Hold globally focused workshops each year to foster the exchange of ideas, results, lessons learned, and best practices from energy systems integration-related activities throughout the world
  • Create and execute a framework for knowledge capture, management, and transfer from energy systems experience and experiments conducted to date and in the future
  • Coordinate, where feasible, institutional investments in future energy systems integration R&D and education to enable efficient and leveraged use of resources.
