European Academies Science Advisory Council Emphasizes Energy Systems Integration

In Europe, energy systems integration is gaining momentum and being recognized as a key enabler of a sustainable energy future. In 2013, the Energy Steering Panel of the European Academies Science Advisory Council advised the European Union of the need for more emphasis on energy systems integration. This and other initiatives have led to energy systems integration being a focal point for the European Strategic Energy Technology Plan Integrated Roadmap, and the development of a joint program in energy systems integration is being proposed within the European Energy Research Alliance.
