Energy Systems Integration Course May 16 and 19 in Dublin

iiESI, in association with Marie Curie Innovative Training Networks and the Energy Institute University College Dublin (UCD), will host a two-day course in energy systems integration (ESI) at UCD on May 16 and May 19, 2016. This course follows on previous successful ESI courses hosted by iiESI.

Download the agenda.

The course is designed for graduate students and practitioners working in the field to give a perspective on the state of the art in ESI. Spaces are limited. To reserve your place now, please email energy@ucd.ie with your name, affiliation, contact details, and how many days you will be attending the course.
