Members and Sponsors

Membership in iiESI is open to any institution or entity actively involved in energy systems integration (ESI)-related research and development, education, or training. iiESI members have access to cutting-edge ESI information and unparalleled student/staff exchanges in prominent ESI organizations worldwide.

Since 2014, the International Institute for Energy Systems Integration (iiESI) has been operating as an informal collaborative group of researchers from premier research institutions and national laboratories across the United States, European Union, and Asia. To date, iiESI has been supported through the financial and in-kind contributions of its participants. Beginning in June 2016, iiESI will formally launch a global, member-driven organization of leading ESI scholars and practitioners to provide a structure for leveraging others' experiences and expertise, coordinating research agendas, and sharing best practices from around the world. The establishment of a formal institute will allow the group to expand and grow to meet the changing needs of the ESI community.

All members who have signed formal membership agreements and made payment of their contributions by June 2016 will be eligible to participate in our first iiESI Members' Meeting, where we will set our work plan for 2016–2017 as well as future events and meeting locations and dates. This meeting will take place in July 2016 in Boston, Massachusetts.

Member Activities and Resources

iiESI members have access to the following activities and resources:

  • Two to three globally focused ESI workshops per year (in rotating locations)
  • Multiple ESI courses and curricula (in person and online)
  • An iiESI online resource library
  • A student and staff exchange program
  • ESI research coordination
  • The iiESI Annual Member Meeting.

Membership Benefits

Members of iiESI can:

  • Participate in the global energy dialogue that informs policy decisions and energy investments
  • Contribute to or participate in leading ESI courses and curricula
  • Have access to world-renowned ESI experts
  • Coordinate research agendas with the world's leading ESI institutions
  • Learn from leading ESI R&D organizations through iiESI courses or student/staff exchanges.

Membership Levels

iiESI is supported by its members through financial contributions. Members may contribute to iiESI and its activities in the following ways:

Affiliate Membership ($10,000 annually)

Affiliate Members have full access to all iiESI resources and the opportunity to host iiESI courses and workshops. Affiliate Members have access to the iiESI student and staff exchange program and are recognized on the iiESI website for their contributions.

Institutional Membership ($50,000 annual contribution)

In addition to all Affiliate Member benefits, Institutional Members receive special consideration for serving on the Executive Committee, which sets the strategic direction of iiESI. Institutional Members are prominently recognized at events, on all promotional materials, and listed on the iiESI website.

Project Sponsors (contribution varies)

Affiliate and Institutional Members have the opportunity to sponsor specific iiESI activities and events, including specific student or staff exchanges.

For more information on iiESI membership or sponsorship, contact Sarah Truitt.
