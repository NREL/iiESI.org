iiESI Asian Workshop

Kyoto International Conference Center
Kyoto, Japan
November 17, 2014

The iiESI Asian Workshop was held November 17, 2014, at the Kyoto International Conference Center in Kyoto, Japan, as part of the International Conference on Integration of Renewable and Distributed Energy Resources.

For an overview of the workshop, see the workshop summary. The workshop agenda and accompanying presentations are provided below.
