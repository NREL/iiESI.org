Join iiESI

Join us in a global collaboration to address energy systems challenges.

Membership in the International Institute for Energy Systems Integration (iiESI) is open to any institution or entity actively involved in ESI-related research and development, education, or training. iiESI members have access to cutting-edge ESI information and unparalleled student/staff exchanges in prominent ESI organizations worldwide.

This coordination enables organizations to learn from each other and maximize the impact of every dollar spent on ESI R&D. The result: accelerated solutions for next-generation energy systems that are flexible, clean, resilient, and reliable.

Membership Levels

iiESI is supported by its members through financial contributions. Members may contribute to iiESI and its activities in the following ways:

  • Affiliate Membership ($10,000 annually)
    Affiliate Members have full access to all iiESI resources and the opportunity to host iiESI courses and workshops. Affiliate Members have access to the iiESI student and staff exchange program and are recognized on the iiESI website for their contributions.

  • Institutional Membership ($50,000 annually)
    In addition to all Affiliate Member benefits, Institutional Members receive special consideration for serving on the Executive Committee, which sets the strategic direction of iiESI. Institutional Members are prominently recognized at events, on all promotional materials, and listed on the iiESI website.

  • Project Sponsors (contribution varies)
    Affiliate and Institutional Members have the opportunity to sponsor specific iiESI activities and events, including specific student or staff exchanges.

Membership Benefits

Members of iiESI will:

  • Chart the course for defining the emerging discipline of ESI
  • Contribute to or participate in leading ESI courses and curricula
  • Have access to world-renowned ESI experts
  • Coordinate research agendas with the world's leading ESI institutions.

In addition, iiESI members have access to the following activities and resources:

  • Multiple globally focused ESI workshops per year (in rotating locations)
  • Multiple ESI courses and curricula (in person and online)
  • An iiESI online resource library
  • ESI research coordination
  • An ESI Annual Meeting.
