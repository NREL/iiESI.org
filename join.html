Membership Levels

iiESI is supported by its members through financial contributions. Members may contribute to iiESI and its activities in the following ways:

Affiliate Membership ($10,000 annually)

Affiliate Members have full access to all iiESI resources and the opportunity to host iiESI courses and workshops. Affiliate Members have access to the iiESI student and staff exchange program and are recognized on the iiESI website for their contributions.

Institutional Membership ($50,000 annually)

In addition to all Affiliate Member benefits, Institutional Members receive special consideration for serving on the Executive Committee, which sets the strategic direction of iiESI. Institutional Members are prominently recognized at events, on all promotional materials, and listed on the iiESI website.

Project Sponsors (contribution varies)

Affiliate and Institutional Members have the opportunity to sponsor specific iiESI activities and events.

Membership Benefits

As a member of iiESI, you will:

  • Access world-renowned ESI experts, leading ESI organizations, and cutting-edge ESI information
  • Gain insights into the emerging field and marketplace
  • Help shape and coordinate ESI research agendas for the world's leading ESI institutions
  • Maximize current R&D investments in ESI
  • Optimize future investments in ESI
  • Strengthen your organization's ESI knowledge, competencies, and offerings
  • Become recognized as an early leader in the new field of ESI.

In addition, iiESI members have access to the following activities and resources:

  • Multiple globally focused ESI workshops per year (in rotating locations)
  • Multiple ESI courses and curricula (in person and online)
  • An iiESI online resource library
  • ESI research coordination
  • An ESI annual meeting.
