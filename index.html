The International Institute for Energy Systems Integration (iiESI) is a global community engaged in developing an efficient global energy system.

iiESI leverages diverse views and expertise to understand the full energy system and the challenges to realizing highly integrated, flexible, clean, and efficient energy systems. iiESI fosters the exchange of ideas, results, lessons, and best practices from energy systems integration activities throughout the world to determine and prioritize actions to transform energy systems that yield the greatest value to the global community.
