What Is Energy Systems Integration?

Also known as ESI, energy systems integration is a holistic view of all energy systems we use today—not just electrical, but also transportation, heat, and water. ESI is about tapping into the combined strength of energy systems—from those that operate in individual homes and communities to those that span whole regions of the world—and maximizing the value of every unit of energy we use.
